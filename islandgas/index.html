Our Story
Carlito and Cesar P. Monastrial brothers shared a vision that resulted in the formation of Island Air Products Corporation.
Now in its 40th year, IAPC continues the entrepreneurial tradition established by its humble beginnings as a small-time trading business selling scraps and junk in the early 1960s. Through a series of unfortunate events and personal challenges, the brother's determination and eagerness to adapt to the ever-changing industry landscape fueled the business's success. Currently, the company's portfolio includes refining plants and warehouses.Satellites, authorized dealers, and retail outlets have grown steadily and show no signs of slowing down as it continues to meet consumers' demand for safety and quality. Island Air Products Corporation, a key leader in the LPG industry, intends to move forward by better engaging its customers and business partners, ensuring they receive the excellent service they deserve.
The iconic yellow steel cylinder with its red logo has become a mainstay in many Filipino households and establishments throughout and beyond Metro Manila, and is poised for further expansion in Visayas and Mindanao.
